Select Genres for Recommendations

If you have not read any manga and are starting from scratch. Select from the drop down menu and pick a genre that you like. You can choose 1 to 3 genres and it will recommend 5 popular manga that contain the genres you selected.

Rate Manga You Have Read

You can enter between 1 and 5 manga that you have read and you can rate each one between 0-10. This will give you a list of 5 manga recommendations based off 30,000 users data. If you don't like your recommendations or what to mix it up, change the ratings and you will get different results.
